Quick guide

How to play

1

Sit side by side

White plays from the bottom (warm squares), Black from the top (blue squares). Share the same screen.

2

Click a piece, then a square

Click any of your pieces to select it - valid moves highlight green. Click a destination to move.

3

Respect the cooldown

After moving, that piece is locked for 3 seconds. Move any other piece freely - each piece is independent.

4

Capture the King to win

No checkmate. Hunt down the enemy King and capture it directly. Game ends immediately.
